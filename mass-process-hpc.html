How It Works: From Photo to Profit

Your Part

01. Capture Bulk Photos

Use a high-resolution camera (i.e. iPhone 17 Pro) to take a single photo of 15-20 items at once. Ensure good lighting and that all barcodes are visible.

Our Part

02. Automated AI Analysis

Our AI + SaaS tool automatically scans the entire frame, identifying every individual object and drawing precise bounding boxes for verification.

Our Part

03. Auto-Cropping & Barcode Reading

The tool cuts each item into its own individual photo. It reads barcodes instantly, or uses AI to estimate the item type and MSRP price if no barcode is found.

Our Part

04. Nice Formatted Manifest

Receive a beautifully formatted manifest with images named by Bin# and Barcode. Everything is ready for instant upload to your auction platform.

Your Part

05. Bin Pickup

Once processing is complete, items are placed in a numbered Bin. You customers can pick them up immediately from your local warehouse.
